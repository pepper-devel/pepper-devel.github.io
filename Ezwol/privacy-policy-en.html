Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Ezwol (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following information only on your device:

  • Host names (user-defined names)
  • MAC addresses (device information for Wake-on-LAN destinations)
  • IP addresses (broadcast addresses or specific IP addresses)
  • Port numbers (ports for sending WOL packets)
  • Notes (user-entered text)
  • Widget configuration information

1.2 External Transmission

The App does not transmit any data, including the above information, to external servers or the internet. All data is stored only on your device and operates completely offline.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Sending Wake-on-LAN packets (to wake up devices on the network)
  • Displaying information on home screen widgets
  • Displaying and managing user-configured information

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

The App does not share user information with any third parties. All data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

4.1 Data Storage

  • All data is stored in a local database on your device
  • Data is not synchronized to the cloud
  • For iOS and Android home screen widgets, selected host information is stored in the app group area

4.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete host information individually within the app
  • All data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Data backup and restore features are not provided

5. Security

Information regarding the App's security:

  • Data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally
  • Network communication is used only for sending Wake-on-LAN packets
  • Access from other apps is restricted by iOS and Android sandbox features

6. Permissions Used

The App uses the following permissions:

  • Network Access: Used to send Wake-on-LAN packets over the network
  • Camera (Optional): Used for the MAC address scanning feature (only when the user uses this feature)
  • Widgets: Used to send WOL packets from home screen widgets

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services or libraries. It operates completely offline and does not communicate with external services.

8. Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect personal information, it can be used safely regardless of age.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you on this page with the update date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please visit the Support page.
